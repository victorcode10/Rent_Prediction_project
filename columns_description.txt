=====================================
NIGERIAN HOUSE RENT DATASET
Column Descriptions
=====================================

1. city
   - Type: Categorical (Text)
   - Values: Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Ibadan, Benin, Ilorin
   - Meaning: The Nigerian city where the property is located
   - Why it matters: Location is the #1 factor affecting rent prices in Nigeria. 
     Lagos and Abuja typically have the highest rents due to being commercial hubs.

2. number_of_bedrooms
   - Type: Numeric (Integer)
   - Values: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
   - Meaning: Total number of bedrooms in the property
   - Why it matters: More bedrooms = bigger property = higher rent

3. number_of_bathrooms
   - Type: Numeric (Integer)
   - Values: 1, 2, 3, 4
   - Meaning: Total number of bathrooms in the property
   - Why it matters: More bathrooms indicate better quality and convenience

4. furnished
   - Type: Categorical (Text)
   - Values: Yes, No
   - Meaning: Whether the property comes with furniture and appliances
   - Why it matters: Furnished apartments command 20-30% premium in Nigerian rental market

5. power_supply_hours
   - Type: Numeric (Integer)
   - Values: 0-24 (hours per day)
   - Meaning: Average hours of electricity supply per day
   - Why it matters: CRITICAL in Nigeria! Properties with 24-hour power supply 
     (generator/inverter/solar) are significantly more expensive

6. security_level
   - Type: Categorical (Text)
   - Values: Low, Medium, High
   - Meaning: Security measures (gates, guards, CCTV, etc.)
   - Why it matters: Security is a major concern. High security areas command premium prices

7. estate_type
   - Type: Categorical (Text)
   - Values: Estate, Non-Estate
   - Meaning: Whether property is in a gated estate or regular neighborhood
   - Why it matters: Estate properties offer better infrastructure, security, and amenities

8. proximity_to_road
   - Type: Categorical (Text)
   - Values: Near, Far
   - Meaning: How close the property is to a main road
   - Why it matters: Properties near main roads have better accessibility and transportation

9. parking_space
   - Type: Categorical (Text)
   - Values: Yes, No
   - Meaning: Whether the property has dedicated parking space
   - Why it matters: Parking is valuable in urban areas; adds to property value

10. monthly_rent (TARGET VARIABLE)
    - Type: Numeric (Integer)
    - Values: Range from ₦50,000 to ₦2,000,000+
    - Meaning: Monthly rental price in Nigerian Naira (₦)
    - Why it matters: This is what we want to predict!
